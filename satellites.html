Satellite List

Here is the list of the satellite names recognized by @WheresThatSat. The list is periodically synchronized with the set of current two-line element sets available from CelesTrak, and as such may change as additions or deletions are made to the CelesTrak catalog.

Aliases

Many satellite names are terse or abbreviated. The following aliases are defined to help @WheresThatSat recognize alternate or expanded forms of certain satellite names. Please send feedback if you have suggestions for additional aliases.

  • Hubble Space Telescope → HST
  • International Space Station → ISS
